== Moku

Moku is a platform for games. Currently it offers tic-tac-toe and snake.

It was built as part of General Assemb.ly WDI and whilst it works and is not recommend for production use.

If you are considering taking the plunge at a immersive bootcamp at General Assemb.ly and would like to speak to somebody about their experience, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
